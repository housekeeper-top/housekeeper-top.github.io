**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy is applicable to the DeMOBA app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Jane R (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider"). This service is provided "AS IS".

**What information does the Application collect?**

The Application **does not collect any personally identifiable information**, and it does not require you to register or sign in to any account.

The data you generate while using the Application — including the game name you enter, the cancellation request time, the length of the cooling-off period, and your daily check-in records — is **stored only locally on your device** using the operating system's standard storage. It is never uploaded to any server, and the Service Provider cannot access it.

**Does the Application collect information automatically?**

No. The Application contains no advertising, integrates no third-party analytics, statistics, or tracking SDKs, and does not collect your device identifiers, IP address, location, or usage behavior. It does not send any data to external services in the background.

**Photo Library Permission**

When you actively tap "Save medal to album", the Application requests permission to add to your photo library, solely to save your "detox succeeded" medal image to your album. The Application does not read, access, or upload any other content from your photo library.

**Third Parties**

The Application does not share data with any third party, because it does not collect data or perform any network transmission.

**Data Retention and Deletion**

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can clear your records inside the app at any time, or simply uninstall the Application to delete all related data.

**Children**

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13, and it does not knowingly collect any information from children.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

The Service Provider may update this privacy policy from time to time. You are therefore advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes will be notified by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-07-07.
