DeMOBA is a focused companion that helps you quit a specific game you keep coming back to. Instead of relying on willpower alone, it turns self-control into a clear, encouraging challenge with a real deadline.

You start by entering the game you want to quit, the moment you filed your account-cancellation request, and the length of the cooling-off period (15 days by default). DeMOBA then shows a live countdown, accurate to the second, so you always know exactly how long is left until the cancellation truly takes effect. As long as you do not reopen the game, the app automatically checks you in every day and offers a warm line of encouragement to help you get through the hardest moments.

When you make it through the entire cooling-off period, DeMOBA generates a personal "detox succeeded" medal that you can save to your photo album as a keepsake of your victory. And if you slip and reopen the game, you can honestly restart the timer and begin again.

DeMOBA keeps everything on your device. The game name, your cooling-off settings, and your check-in records are stored locally with the system's standard storage. The app contains no ads, collects no personal data, and sends nothing to any server.
