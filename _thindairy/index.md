The application aims to help users to record weight datas by AI technology. The user interface is simple and easy to be operated. When users click the scanning button, the datas shown on the electronic scales can be recorded quickly, conveniently, and efficiently. For recorded datas, users can modify, download, delete, etc. The recorded datas will be presented in the form of charts in the chart function. So the users can feel the changes of weight visually. Usually, the recorded datas will be strictly kept confidential. Other personalized designs can be made in the settings.

The application is suitable for users who are currently losing weight to record their weights. Of course, it is also a good choice for users who have a need of weight recording or care about their health.

 If there are obvious effects, users can see changes every day. After using the application for a period of time, the users may have different experiences.
